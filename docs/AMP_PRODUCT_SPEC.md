# AMP Product Spec

## Product Definition
AMP (All of My Points) is a private, trust-based decision support service that helps clients make better point and travel decisions with explainable outputs.

## AI Roles
- Perplexity: research support for public-source fact gathering.
- ChatGPT: structure and synthesis of client-facing report narratives.
- Gemini: verification pass for consistency and risk flagging before delivery.

## Report Principles
- Evidence: recommendations should trace to public, verifiable sources.
- As-of date: each report states a clear `asOf` date.
- Disclaimer: policy or offer changes can occur without notice.
- Client ownership: final decision authority remains with the client.

## 3-Tier Model
- Essential: single best option suggestion, no audio, no chatbot.
- Strategic: up to three options, no audio, no chatbot.
- Executive: up to three options, includes audio, includes 7-day chatbot access.

## Executive Chatbot
Executive tier includes a 7-day report-based chatbot window. The chatbot should remain grounded in the delivered report and linked evidence.
